About

I am aiming to master software engineering, mathematics, and data science in order to create better things. My interests are probabilistic machine learning, ring theory, and data structure algorithms.

I was born in Mashhad, Iran in January 1999. Currently, I am pursuing a BS in Mathematics & Computer Science at Temple University located in Philadelphia.

On my free time, I play the piano and the guitar while I study music theory. I also try to read philosophy, psychology, and economy related books. Sometimes I like to design. More specifically I like to design websites and develop them.
